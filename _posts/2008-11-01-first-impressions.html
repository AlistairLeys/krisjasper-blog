HEY EVERYONE!

I feel really, really tired today. We went home nearly 2am, and I slept at around 4am. It has been a long day yesterday. But it was fun coz the staff team went out last night (@ Broadstreet again, where else?) and A met us at around 12am. More of it next week.

I also feel like I haven't been in here for a long time. I know I have posted those photos of snow last Thursday, but looking at it now, I feel like it has actually complicated the identity of my site. I thought this is Krisjasper.com and not emote-with-nature.com (I'm sorry if that site actually exists, I only typed it in random). lol. I started my site as my life's online journal coz I'm a nerd, but after more than 370 days, it has become a variety show! lol. I have done camwhoring, poetries, started a book, video blogging, cooking show, travel blog, photography, given advices, changed my name, met my twins, and God knows what else I have done (and will be doing)!

My last 2 posts havent really said anything about the recent things in my life... Well, nothing has changed...

I still inhale oxygen and exhale carbon dioxide (sometimes, I experiment and do it other way around),

I still go to work and eat sweets,

I still go to the gym to perform self inflicted pain coz I think I enjoy it,

Basically I'm still the same. WEIRD AND BORING.

I cant believe it's the first of November already. Where did 2008 go?!? Speaking of FIRST, lemme conclude my series of FIRSTS this week (please look at the title of my posts this week).

Yesterday was the first time I lifted free weights in the gym (barbels). It was my first time to realize that 30kgs in machines is 100% lighter than free weights. My arms were shaking! A was there to assist me, which made it look more ridiculous coz I looked like I was in my first day in school with my Nanny accompanying me! Then last night was the first time I went into that buffet resto, then first time for A to join us.

So to conclude my first series of Firsts (huh?!) I will publish this...

We slept on the sofa last night coz we had 2 visitors; when I woke up I was inspired to take photos of myself (HELLO! who else?) and 3 of my pets. I have more than a dozen of "pet" dogs but I will show only them 3 for now.

Meet Husky. He was given to me by A on our first Christmas together.




Meet Zooey. When A and I went to London Zoo last 2003, we adopted her.


And this is Fayre. I won her (actually cheated) when I went to Pinoy Fair. There was this game where you hook those floating plastic ducks out of the pond and you get the prize written on their bottom if you are lucky. Well, what I did, I swayed those ducks (when the old fair lady wasnt looking) so that I'd know if there was a prize written under them. If there's no prize I'd put them back on water, until I hooked one plastic duck that has the code for a stuffed toy, hence I brought Fayre home. Fayre just love that story, the story of how she became part of this family.



I have been watching a lot of US elections/FOXnews recently, and I'm getting worried that my friend Palin might not make it to the Whitehouse. Sigh....



also, I have been eating a lot lately... sigh....


And the days gone shorter and cold. It's freezing! Sigh.....


A bought another laptop. We might convert our apartment into a call centre/office. Well, with 2 lappies, one desktop, 3 phones that can access the net I think it is feasible.



Speaking of mobiles, I cant wait for N96's price to go down. I doubt if they'll do it before the Holidays (you know, season of giving)... But the only way I could have N96 for free is if I agree to sign for an 18 month contract, and Im not a fan of 18months contract. Hopefully Just after Xmas they'll offer it for free even in 12 months contract.
Sigh....


I love this site: playonclick.com.

OK now, I need a white neck tie.. I've been planning to go out to purchase one since I woke up But Im so tired and sleeeeeeepy. Maybe later...



GoodByE for now,
HAPPY HALLOWEEN EVERYONE!
