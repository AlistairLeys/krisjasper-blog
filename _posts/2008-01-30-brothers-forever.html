--- layout: post title: "Brothers Forever" date: 2008-01-30 ---


We went to East Midlands (Derby) the other day. My friend Jill had invited me to visit her as I haven't really seen her (nor contacted.. hehe) since December 2004. Silly coz Derby is only 45 mins via train from Birmingham. We just lost contact.


Jill has been a good friend and a colleague since we reviewed for the local board. When we worked in the hospital, the shift request book was always filled with "same sked please" for the two of us. Let us just say that the shifts were more fun if you work with friends (and playing cards and cooking in ICU). LOLs.


I thought Life is mostly about freaking deadlines and slavery @ work, until I see old friends again and recognize Life more as a playground.
Sometimes you play happy, and at other times you go home crying.
Sometimes you play in groups,
At times you play on your own, solitary...
Happiness in Life is not about how many you have when you're on top,
but how many had stayed when you're down in the dumps.
