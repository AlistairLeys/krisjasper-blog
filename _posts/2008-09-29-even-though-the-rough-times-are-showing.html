Produced by Whittard, given by boss coz I am charity.

And on that Thursday I wore that shirt that I bought in Uniqlo London and my FCUK jacket.


And on that Thursday while wearing that Uniqlo shirt we received a letter from CSCI saying that we no longer have to be inspected coz (the management team is the bestest looking in the country), err... my boss had sent a document that evidenced us to be a good Nursing home.
That's our "Happy face"


That's my "very happy" face.


And instantaneously it was Sunday. And on that Sunday I wore that shirt I bought from Chicago.


And that USC cropped trousers, and TopMan shoes.
That's a fake smile. Aint feeling well yesterday. If you're a regular reader you'd know why..
If you're a non-regular reader, please read my previous post.
If you're my regular reader, you have good taste and possibly good looking...
If you're a non-regular reader, you know now what to do to have good taste and be possibly good looking?

Then just before 10pm, boss foned; Boss said if I could wear a brown thing tomorrow (we always colour coordinate), coz she'll be wearing brown. We have people to interview so we should look like normal, real people.
I said ok.
Then I go home and I see A, and I pretend Im strong,
  • I pretend to converse with A
  • I pretend everything's ok now with A (there's a lot you dont know)
  • I pretend I am not hungry
  • I pretend I am not thirsty
  • I pretend I inhale oxygen
  • I pretend I exhale CO2.
  • I pretend I feel sleepy.
  • I pretend I wake up ok (there's a lot you dont know).
And on this assumably very happy day, I pretend I am a normal person working on a normal office hours.
I pretend I am a normal person who had woken up from a normal 7 hours sleep.
I greet this 3rd planet from the sun with a pretentious smile. It's a new week afterall, let me start it right. So I went to our kitchen cupboard and prepared my pills cocktail.
Then I am ready.
And on this assumably very happy Monday morning I wore that brown thingy from River Island and my black scarf from Gap.






















Then because we pretend we were busy on this Monday morning, we pretend we needed a break. (and oh, Im glad I saw my vegan friend again today... that's a good thing coz she inspired me to be vegan again.... I might pretend to start tomorrow).
Have you seen the new Nokia N96? I might miss my N95 soon.
That's boss, me and Babs... Babs joined the paralympics last month for having a curly hair.

Make Us A Supermodel U.K.S.A.

Then after my pretend to be normal shift, I went to the City centre and looked for that DVD you guys had recommended me (Across the Universe). So I went to HMV, but I felt dizzy so I just bought a Sesame street shirt from them and went home. I will order that thing in Amazon later.
Anyway, coz I pretend to be a normal human today, let me eat normally. That's my tea, New York Cheesecake.. SEE??? I do eat.
I will go to ________ on Wednesday, with A.
A doesnt want me to tell anyone where we are going and what we are going to do.
A is weak.
and I pretend to be strong.
