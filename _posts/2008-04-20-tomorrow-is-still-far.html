kailan ba darating ang bukas para sa akin?
(when will my tomorrow come?)

Yesterday was Saturday, I wore a grey shirt, twisted skinny Topman jeans and a green/white TopMan athlete's jacket. My Boss who worked yesterday morning said I looked like an athlete.. some staff said I looked like I'm still in high school..

I went home and asked A what I looked like...

A said just "OK".

Just OK? People thought I came out from a catalogue, and A said just OK.

The staff said I looked really well, and A said just OK.

I looked in the mirror and I looked like I could still join TEEN PBB, and A said just OK?

I dont think it's ok.


AT ISA PANG EKSPLOSIBONG REBELASYON:
(& Another explosive revelation)

Sunday. I again worked this morning.

After seven and a half hours, I informed my boss that I'm staying for a bit (not as overtime). Coincidentally there is something that I need to install in one of our nursing home's computer anyway and that was done. But that wasn't really the reason why I stayed and didn't go home yet. We now have a videoke machine in the nursing home coz Boss thought she could be the next Simon Cowell and discover raw talents. While fiddling with the computer, I was listening to some staff singing.

HOLY COW!

I thought Godzilla was in the house! It was really scary, er, funny. I found the videoke machine really cool though. It has a built in cam and is connected to the TV, so obviously you'll see yourself while you're singing and you could pretend you're in American Idol waiting to be criticised by the three stooges, er, judges.

When I thought that I had listened enough to those crazy staff's singing, and my brain couldn't handle it anymore, I went to my locker, had one capsule of Ibuprofen and left.

AT ISA PANG NAGBABAGANG REBELASYON
(another hot revelation)

It's freezing in this country for the last few days. I haven't been to the gym since Wednesday. I'm trying to lose weight but Im too lazy to walk to the gym coz it's so cold. So I'm just starving myself til maybe tomorrow. I want to look as white as snow and anorexic when I go to the US on June. I want to look like a goth.


Malayo pa ang umaga...
(tomorrow's still far...)
