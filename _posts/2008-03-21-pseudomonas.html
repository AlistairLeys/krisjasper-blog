--- layout: post title: "PSEUDOMONAS" date: 2008-03-21 ---

Get over it. As a regular reader, you should no longer be surprised why I titled this as such.

My parcel did not arrive yesterday. Today and Monday are bank holidays due to Lent and Easter so no mail deliveries on these days. I'm hoping it'll arrive tomorrow.

I went to work yesterday afternoon, went home at 10pm, me and "A" just ate pancit canton and garlic bread, I had a shower, we then started to play the DVD (RUN fatboy RUN), after 5 minutes we fell asleep while it was still playing. We just wasted electricity. Shame... Please blame the boring film.

This morning Discovery Channel showed 3 docus.
"Search of Eden", "The real King Herod", and "Who is Moses". I learn more if I watch documentaries. I will not tell you what those shows were about. With my interpretation, I might alter history by mistake. It would be easier (and safer) if you just watch them.
After watching them, I went to the gym. Wrong time to go, it was packed. I should've gone there earlier today. I thought I'll just do cardio for 40 minutes then I'll go, but luckily some machines were free after so I decided to stay. The machine I used for my arms was facing another machine and old gorilla-man was using it. That area was a bit dim, and all I could see were his big white eyes looking at me. Maybe I'm jealous that he has big eyes and mine are chinky, maybe I'm jealous that he could lift nearly 70kg and I can barely lift 30 (I'm just toning anyway).. I dont know.. But I always feel uncomfortable if somebody's in front of me, more so if somebody's looking at me. After 60 minutes in the gym, I left.

We'll have sardines in sunflower oil, mushroom fried rice, crispy salad with sweetcorn and pizzas tonight. Except for the salad, I'll just shove them all in the oven. I'm too lazy to cook. I need to finish my reviewer, the sooner the better.

I'm busy this weekend so you might hear from me on Easter Monday already.

Happy Easter Everyone! Au Revoir!
