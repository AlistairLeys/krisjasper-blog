"Search of Eden", "The real King Herod", and "Who is Moses". I learn more if I watch documentaries. I will not tell you what those shows were about. With my interpretation, I might alter history by mistake. It would be easier (and safer) if you just watch them.

Maybe I'm jealous that he has big eyes and mine are chinky, maybe I'm jealous that he could lift nearly 70kg and I can barely lift 30 (I'm just toning anyway).. I dont know.. But I always feel uncomfortable if somebody's in front of me, more so if somebody's looking at me. After 60 minutes in the gym, I left.
