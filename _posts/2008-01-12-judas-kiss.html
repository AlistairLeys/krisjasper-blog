Have you ever kissed somebody but didn't really mean it?

But Judas Kiss is not just about kissing... it could be in forms of phone calls, text, or a simple conversation.

There are people I cant stand, but I still answer their calls, reply their emails, text them back...

Am I being a fake? Or just being civil?

There are staff I cant stand as well. They're so quick in criticising their colleagues, not realizing that they've not proven their worth at work yet.

Who are they kidding?
