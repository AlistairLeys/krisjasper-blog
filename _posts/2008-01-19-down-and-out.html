My life has been blatantly boring this week.

There's just nothing to write..

My Boss was on leave though, so I was the responsible person for the Nsg Home.

I only phoned staff every shift, to cover 170 hours of absences (altogether for the week... bummer!).

I only met people from our Head Office and the Government.

they visited the Nsg Home...

stayed for a couple of hours...

toured and inspected, looked at our files...

and nothing unusual, they were SIMPLY impressed
by the Nsg Home, and my charm, of course.
LOLz...

But the credit should go to my team who worked really hard yesterday.
It was an outstanding effort.

Well Done EveryOne!!!

That was our most significant contribution to the British Society. hehe...
_____________________

Speaking of this week, together I have processed my British residency
and US documents.

I hope to receive my passport from the UK Home Office within a week or two,
and I am flying to the US by summer...
Only for two weeks though.
& I might move there by the year's end,
or might not.
I dont know...
I really have difficulty in deciding...
I think it's the thought that I will leave a secured life here,
and start from shit again.

Au Revoir EveryOne!
