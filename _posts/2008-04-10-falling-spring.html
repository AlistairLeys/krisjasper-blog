--- layout: post title: "Falling Spring" date: 2008-04-10 ---

For Gwen, who loved and fought life too long (1927-2008).


What a day for day dream
What a day for day dreamin' boy
And I’m lost in a daydream
Dreamin ‘bout my bundle of joy


And even if time ain’t really on my side
It’s one of those days for takin' a walk outside



I’m blowin’ the day to take a walk in the sun
And fall on my face on somebody's new mowed lawn

I've been havin’ a sweet dream
I been dreamin’ since I woke up today


It’s starrin’ me and my sweet dream
Cause she's the one that makes me feel this way


And even if time has passed me by a lot

I couldn't care less about the dues you say I got

Tomorrow I'll pay the dues for droppin’ my load


A pie in the face for bein’ asleep before dawn


And you can be sure that if you're feelin’ right

A daydream will last along into the night


Tomorrow at breakfast you can pick up your ears


Or you may be daydreamin for a thousand years


What a day for day dream


What a day for a daydreamin' boy.
