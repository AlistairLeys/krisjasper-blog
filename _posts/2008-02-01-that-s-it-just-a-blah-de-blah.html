--- layout: post title: "THAT'S IT? just a blah de blah" date: 2008-02-01 ---

Since the start of the week, mostly everyone was saying

"It'll rain on Thursday and we'll have HEAVY snow on Friday"

Yesterday was Thursday, it was freezing... yeah!

But I can't remember if it rained.

Today is Friday.. well, the weather was ok this morning...

I managed to go to Bullring as FCUK was

having a good sale...

I looked at the sky, still nice and sunny.

After a couple of hours, I went back home...

then I decided to go to the gym.

I looked at the sky... still nice and sunny.

Hmmm...

I decided to make a vegetable soup for my supper...

so I dropped by @ Sainsbury's to buy leek, onion, carrots, mixed beans, pasta and veggie meat.

Just as I was unloading my groceries... I've heard a strong gush of wind!

Hmmm...

I looked at the sky, It's already hazy...

And it's freezing!

Hmmm....

Yehey! the snow is here! You see, Snow makes me happy!

They even said we'll have blizzards!

But when I looked out the window, it was hailing... not snowing...

I didnt care! I thought, everything will still turn white!

But after 30 secs, it stopped!

THAT'S IT?

Where's the HEAVY SNOW?

Who gave the false effing weather report again?

I was waiting for the snow, and what did we have?

Dandruffs on the road?

a small peck of ice stones?

was that the HEAVY thing? Hell No!

It looked like an elf just made a wee wee!

THAT'S IT?

as Catherine Tate would say...

What a F*cking Liberty!
____________________

We watched SuperBad in DVD last nite.

There was no morale in the story,

but I could relate to the plot.

There are things that I can't tell some of my friends

as I don't want them to feel upset.

Might as well keep it to myself, than disappoint them.

Au Revoir Everyone!
