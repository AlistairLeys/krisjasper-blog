The moon gives Earth its climatic stability. Without the moon there will be no seasons, making our planet change its temperatures frequently and rapidly, from hot to freezing.

The moon is also responsible for the tides. Giving the early forms of life on Earth the chance to live on land, from the sea... that's why Earth has a complex life.

But the moon is drifting away from us, approximately 4 cm per year.

Similarly, I feel that the chances of Us Forever is slowly drifting away.

Without my moon, who'd give me stability? Who'd give me life?
