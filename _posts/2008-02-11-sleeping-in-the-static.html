--- layout: post title: "Sleeping In The Static" date: 2008-02-11 ---

I have always been so miserable and depressive,
then life throws me a good hand
and everything seems so much better...

I was at work yesterday. All freaking day...

It was Sunday, so it was a bit boring.

After doing what I thought I should be doing, I stayed in the lounge, with the other staff while flicking my mobile... They thought I was listening to whatever it was they were talking, but I was actually busy editing the pics in my phone...
An occassional "Oh, Yeah.." and "mmm.. ok..." and they thought I was really interested with their discussions.
But as always, I was in a world of my own.

Some people just talk about other people, at times to forget their own life.
SAD.

Boss is already back. So I asked one of the staff to help me put some welcome back decors and balloons in her office.
I know... I'm such a creep... Well, I'm her Deputy. I'm earning nearly £--,---.00 a year to do it. LOL.
And besides, I dont wanna feel guilty when she gives me my gift from abroad (ahem!).
Oh, she said there was no shop near their hotel... yeah, ryt!

It has been really sunny for the last 5 days. Still chilly, but at least I can wear my sunglasses again. I love dark,gloomy weathers but after having it for quite awhile, I do miss the sun now.
I might ask the sun to marry me so that she wont go away again.
That reminds me... my cousin promised to take me to Wisconsin again on Summer. That's the good thing with Illinois, as it' a small land area, you can just drive to other states. Unlike when Mary drove me from San Diego to Las Vegas, I thought I travelled from Birmingham to Dubai again. hehe...

I'm on my 4th month of blogging now. I started checking my hits only 2 months ago, and not bad.. nearly 4,600. My clustrmap's reflecting an international bothered readers.
I have friends everywhere, but I dont know who went to Eastern Europe...
and to Africa...
and to Alaska... (well, it's showing in the clustrmap)
Please, introduce yourself. I'd like to thank you personally, even via an email.

Good Bye!
