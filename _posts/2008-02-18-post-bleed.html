So how was your Valentine?

I was working all day that day... We also interviewed nurses that'd be joining our team. So in the PM I was already mentally and physically tired (of questioning and talking).

That is me, just checking my hair. Look at how I was trying to make my eyes bigger. I've been practicing that ever since I discovered that my eyes are a bit smaller in photos, and after 2 decades, I'm nearing perfection.

I dont know the name of the belt, but that's from a staff who went to India and she gave me that. Here I am again, just playing around (Boss, I know you're reading this, but I was actually checking if I still look presentable for the visitors or any unannounced inspections, you know....)

Lemme just share this recipe.. It's actually prawns cooked in cream and chillis. It's really hot, which I like. This was our appetizer last V-day.



Yesterday, BK (staff) said she feels her death is coming. I said that would be impossible coz no one could predict it. I then asked these two staff if they're afraid of death. One said Yeah, and BK said No.
Me, I'm not afraid of dying. I'm more afraid that it would be the people I love who will die first.


(Warning. This is me talking DeeP) This is a bit sensitive, but when I attended mass last Saturday, after I thanked God for always giving me blessings and all the trials He had given me are really just shallow (Coz I'm sure He knows I am shallow), I asked myself... What if we die, we just die and that's it? We turn to dust and that's it?

Please, do not challenge me.. I am Catholic, I will never change my religion, but I am also entitled to my own thinking and opinions. I am not heretic. When I asked BK that yesterday, she said No, coz all of us will live forever after we die. But I told her that billions of years from now, the sun will consume all it's fuel, that means we will all die anyway.. I watched this show about the end of the universe. Imagine the universe as a balloon, coz it's continously expanding, time will come it will explode. I dont think BK got my concept coz she talked about Noah's Arc then. This is the kind of conversation you'll have if you talk about Science and Religion. Deep and Boring.

I apologize for the above post. I am reminding you all that you're still in www.krisjasper.com and this site is all about my random rant and rambling. You are not in geekyfreak.com.
