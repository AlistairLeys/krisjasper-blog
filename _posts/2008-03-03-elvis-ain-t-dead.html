That's my fave song now. It's from a band called SCOUTING FOR GIRLS. I listened to it 100 times today. And still counting...


I slept at 3am and woke up at 7:30am. I couldn't maintain sleep. I already explained in my previous post why. I feel crappy again, in addition to my depression, I'm struggling with my NCLEX book and my test is just a couple of months away.

After replying to your comments, I thought of going to the gym before 930am (again, I also explained before why I dont want to go to the gym after 10am). But I felt lazy to go to the gym today. So to inspire me to shed off some pounds, I went to our kitchen, ate one muller rice, then I consumed the stir fried noodles with Thai curry sauce that "A" stored in the fridge (our left over from last nite), then a slice of apple pie, a small Milky Way bar and a scoop of Ben and Jerry's Cookie Dough ice cream. After practicing glutony, I felt like a dumb ham... I was then ready to go to the gym.

Going home I bought these items for our tea.


I will compensate to what I ate this morning. I'll add that avocado to the salad. "A" requested if I could buy a crispy salad . ( I dont like crispy salad but I'm pacifying "A" at the minute, so the less arguments we'll have, the better for my existence.. let's leave it that way.).

I also have this idea of tearing the pages of my NCLEX reviewer and eat them for my tea. I'm wondering if my stomach will transmit to my shrinking brain the contents of the page. That way, I dont need to study and I will not gain weight as well.

Before reaching my place, I've experienced all FOUR seasons again in a span of 4 hours. It was sunny but chilly when I went out of our flat, then it rained just when I finished in the gym. Windy (with small ice droppings) when I was buying those vegetables, and now sunny again. British weather ne'er cease to amaze me.

Crap. I might have some sleeping pills tonight.
