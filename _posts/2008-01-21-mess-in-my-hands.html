--- layout: post title: "Mess In My Hands" date: 2008-01-21 ---

"Keep your friends close, and your enemies closer"

I grew up living that advice.

Key to success is knowing your competition,

and strike them in their achilles' heel.

"The enemy of my enemy is my friend."

In this planet, you can only either be

a Winner or a Loser.

Your choice.

Life will never be fair.
