I went back to work yesterday afternoon, after having four days off.

I always feel awkward going back to work after a few days off.

But if I dont have a few days off, I feel awkward as well.

I dont think my mind knows where to put me.

Well, when I entered my work place yesterday, it was so silent.

and that's really unusual...

The lights started flickering when I went in... creepy.

I was dyspneic, I knew something's wrong... The atmosphere wasn't just right.

I was wondering if I'll be attacked and murdered by someone as soon as the door closed behind me.

HELP!

But then one staff (MT) shouted my name and gave me a hug when I was approaching the office.

and another one (YL) went there to hug me as well.

Aww... I wont be murdered then.

But then one staff just walked pass me.

And another went in the office to leave her bag, but didn't ask how my days off were!

Didn't they know I was off for four days?

Didn't they know I wasn't working for the last four days?

Didn't they know I truly enjoyed my four days off?

Aren't they interested how my four days were?

Or maybe these two are planning my murder?

HELP!

God, I'm so bored with work, I might start ruining other staff's lives in there.
LOL.
