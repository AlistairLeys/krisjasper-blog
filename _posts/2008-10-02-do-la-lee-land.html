What A Wonderful Day


The fish is flying and birds are swimming,
The cows join the herd,
The lions are scared.
The serpents play with children
Together with the bunnies
Which voluntarily go
Inside the serpents’ head.


The cockroaches are jumping
It’s about time to see sunshine they say…
But unfortunately the scorpions
Also made their way
And Sting! Someone had to pay.


Horses no longer run away
And they are left alone.
Also Elephants can now fly,
They even jump and squash a passer-by
With their tusks and mighty bones.


My spirit has died
And auctioned in E-bay
My soul has been stolen
And sold in Amazon.co.uk
I am actually slowly waning away
But it’s ok as I have my Valium to play.


If only all of us live in do-la-lee land
Sing the songs from the heart
Shout and dance are remarked
As if accompanied by a majestic band.
Stir-fry and barbeque, the noodles are cooked
I have not seen a queue this long
Since I said I will expose my cock.


Why is it that you are so pretty?
You come in different sizes and colours.
I can just put you all in my hand
And down swallow your concoction with a glass of water.
You are all my friends
Don’t listen to whatever they might say
You always, always have helped me
Yesterday, Tomorrow and Today.
