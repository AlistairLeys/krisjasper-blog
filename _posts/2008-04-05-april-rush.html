I started my week with depression, I'm ending it with a cold. Yes, again! It's ONLY a cold but it is annoying me.
I was reading the side effects of my uppers and they're mostly drowsiness, lethargy. That's weird coz those tablets should make me happy and jump for joy like a maniac, and should NOT make me sleep. Is that what you call "conflict of interests"?
That white chocolate above was one of my 2007 Christmas gifts. I didn't have the heart to break his cute little head and smash his smile and devour his body, but I thought to myself that worshiping a tiny white chocolate isn't healthy so I destroyed him. And the vultures ate him in 3 record breaking minutes.
Yesterday was friday, and again I was confused whether to wear jeans as it's nearly weekend anyway, or wear something that covers 90% of my body (like most of the weekdays) and expose only my hands, neck and head. I wore jeans at first and topped it with a casual shirt, but I realized that some therapists are visiting the home so I changed again... That tiny, worthless dilemma in my small universe resulted to lateness.
I went to a friend's 10th wed anniv party last night. Hello to Gladys, Israel and Praise! Gladys' s a workmate and a regular reader of my placid, regular life. I dont know whether it's because of the weather changing, or someone was an incubus of some plague and still decided to attend. Anyhow, the end result? I have been sneezing all night and now my nose is dripping like a faucet. That's a leche flan below.

Enjoy the weekend!

And yeah, watch out for my next post.

It'll DEFINITELY CHANGE your lives...

Well I hope, coz my pathetic life did!
