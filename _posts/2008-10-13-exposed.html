AND...










This is KRIS JASPER actually talking:
Why did I start blogging in the first place? I dont know. But I have enjoyed it, and still enjoying it. I am just appreciating the fact that so far, my readers have accepted what I regularly offer.


Also, through blogging I have met new friends, all sorts really. Photographers, composers, artists, models, travellers, Doctors, colleague nurses, vegetarians, students, Emos, Fashionistas, fellow OFWs, call centre workers, Jokers, lawyers, advocates, story tellers, etc... These humans have welcomed me in their sites (which I prefer to call home), and they have unselfishly shared with me their new projects, recent news, photos, advices.

For the last 12 months, luckily, nobody has ridiculed me, nor insulted me for all the weirdness I have published. To name a few: My pictures, my diet, my medications, my health, my thoughts, my rants, my tastes... Basically, my life really.

And as I am still hoping to settle to one specific identity, I have already been called everything. I am Insomniac, I am probably bipolar, I am mysterious, I am sometimes rude, I am just a piece of meat, I am elegant, I am arrogant, I am weird, I am passionate, I KNOW I AM HOT (lol), I am violent, I am recently a drunkard, they say I am a bully and arsey (the series has not ended yet). But more than anything...

I AM JUST A SHALLOW BASTARD WHO WILL POST ANYTHING.


Coz I am KRIS JASPER.
And I dont give a shit.
