This is the first time Im showing my baby sister (and my bro-in-law) in this site.

As much as I'd like to maintain the anonymousity of my private life (you know, I dont want my global readers to think that because I am pathetic, sedative dependent and forced to inhale oxygen and exhale CO2 everyday, those people who share the same clusters of DNA with me also do the same).

No, my sister and my dad, and some of our relatives are normal. That's why they are boring (LOL!!!).

My sis has moved to St. Maarten (somewhere in the Carribean), and understandably, as it is the first time she's away of the Philippines, she is a little bit (?) sad. And no, she's not a pirate, she's a nurse.

As a good (looking) brother, I thought of helping her cope with depression, not by taking Prozac (because that is mine) , but through this...

HER OWN PHOTOS.
365 DAYS OF THAT?

Sis, you're lucky that you have your husband with you when you moved.
Remember, I was alone when I moved in UK.

365 days of that?
I will share with you the advice I got when I moved here,
"If you feel low, dont isolate yourself... GO OUT! Walk around!"
6 years ago, when I moved here in the UK, my first address was in a farm. Yes, farm.
My 2nd address was still in a farm.
And when my agent's moved me here in Birmingham... I thought they moved me in India, but it's ok. I coped with it.
"Shubha prabhaat. Apabhransha kalidas's vikramorvashiyam. Dharasena valabhi's apabhramsha. Mahabharata, sultan kudarat"
sorry, got carried away.

You have 365 days of that?


365 days of that?

365 days of that?
