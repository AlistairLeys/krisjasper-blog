Somebody thought I work in TopMan!!!

TopMan people are like the skinny versions of A&F.

Well, what can I say?....

LOL.

Maybe she thought:

1. I look like a model (ahem!)

2. I look skinny (ahem!)

3. There was just nobody around she could ask, and because I looked scruffy (which unfortunately I think is the trademark of those who work there.. in a trendy way though)
she thought I work there (Hell, No!)

Seriously, those who know me know that I love chocolates...

a. spoonful of my fave choco spread (yes.)- I'll lick the spoon for that!

b. choco drinks (yes.)

c. choco bars (of course. yes.)

d. chocolate covered fish and chips (err... no.)

And they're wondering how come I really don't go plumpy.
They thought I'm doing something absurd.
Well, lemme check...
This morning I had hot choco and chocolate flapjack for breakfast, then @ 10am I had mushrooms, beans, french toast & tomatoes @ work (and then I ate chocolate filled croissant just after that), then just before my shift finished, I had 1/2 of Galaxy bar.


What they're not actually aware of is, I only pig out on chocolates once a week. They do not realize that on the other 6 days, I eat sensibly. Ok, I honestly eat chocolates daily, but I dont consume the whole fucking bar. In between, I eat fruits, or I don't take carbo in my main meal if I had a choco bar. I simply compensate.
I'm not saying this is appropriate, maybe it's not.. but who cares?
It works for me. Plus, I go to the gym, so I definitely burn out more calories..

I'm not anorexic, NOR BULIMIC... That is simply ridiculous!

My friend Jamie Oliver said that it's ok to eat Dark chocolates..

It's good for your heart apparently.

Au Revoir Everyone.
