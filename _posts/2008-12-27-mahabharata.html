--- layout: post title: "MAHABHARATA" date: 2008-12-27 ---

yeah, Im writing my longest post, or something like that.

This was me, days before Christmas.

As if someone had written what specification and features a "nearly" perfect specimen should have and that someone had decided to produce me.

In here I am wearing a real skinny jeans, a purple jumper and everything else that does matter to any shallow individual.


And at Christmas eve, after arguing with A as A didnt want to buy me my most favourite food in the whole wide world since I was a toddler, and my most favourite food in the whole wide world is TWIGLETS.
But later on as A knows I am a descendent of Vikings, that my ancestors had done crimes in the ancient world and it's just a matter of time and petty triggers and violence will be resurrected in this world, that I could afford to ruin anyone's Christmas, A had decided to buy me 2 tumblers of TWIGLETS only if I buy A a bouquet of flowers .
Then like an ice left in the park under the scorching heat of the sun, I mellowed. I melted... My furies had died. I had my TWIGLETS... A had those bouquet of flowers.... and we are freakingly happy.

But as if my obstacles to endless Nirvana weren't enough, I forgot that I am KrisJasper, and that I ate papers. That I am different, that I always fight the tide and face opposing directions. 48 hours ago (or more if you happen to read this on 28th of December, or much even later) I sinned. A "forced" me to eat these:

  • ROASTED PORK CHOPS seasoned with garlic and soy sauce and brushed with butter
  • prawns fried in olive oil
  • sprouts that had deadly side effects
  • Spag with sweet and chilli tomato sauce, with 2.5 minced beef and topped with cheese
  • Fruit salad made of Caramelized Dulce de Leche, pulverized Marks and Spencer's meringue, thick/double cream, Fruits, nata de coco and everything else that could end the life of Diabetics.
  • Fruit cake topped with cherries, raisins and nuts
  • and my Dad's recipe: whole duck cooked in 7up, wine, pineapple juice, soysauce and topped with pineapple slices, with baby tatos at the side (but A thought it's nice to ruin it by adding those vegetables). I reminded A that it should look like a freaking duck with syrup, not a fucking duck stew. But too late... the murder has been done.

Some of my Christmas 2008 presents... I didnt want to show this as I'd look like materialistic. I just cant...
and A called me materialistic ( well, since my bday...) But I dont mind coz I agree.


and drawings from my youngest reader... Praise. (thank you Praise)



And after those physical and emotional injuries, the gods had punished me and turned me into this:



I watched "Twilight" with A last night. I told A that now, I want to be a vampire. But first, I want to know what white thing it is that makes their face white. Was it Chin chun su? I want to be a vampire less chin chun su.

So since we got home,
  • I have been talking with a deep toned voice,
  • Talked using flowery/ancient english words (aka: neologism; Neologism are usually practiced by schizos),
  • I decided I would stop taking my pills as my pills make me sleepy... and vampires dont sleep
  • I bite A's neck everytime I feel the urge. A said I have to be careful as a dagger will be embedded in my heart if I dont stop. (I have not told A that I dont have a heart).
So I am a schizophrenic vampire less chin chun su, less sleeping pills.

this is Kris Jasper actually talking:

Happy Birthday to my baby sister! I love you and you know I wont hesitate to drop everything in this world for you.
Well... as long as that thing is not an expensive, antique, porcelain china.

Love you Ching.
