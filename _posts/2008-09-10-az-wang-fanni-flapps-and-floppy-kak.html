After work, my friend Tony met me in Bullring as I needed to buy a lilac jumper from Selfridges (well I thought I needed it).

Tony asked me if I really need it.

I asked Tony if I asked for his opinion.

Then we went to River Island and I saw that amazing yellow, rebel scarf my innocent eyes had ever seen. And from that first glance I knew I needed it too. That scarf was waving its cotton fibers to me, telling me to pick him up, purchase him and we will start a beautiful journey together.

And that scarf is now hidden in my drawer.... resting. He will be needing all that energy once I start wearing him. This planet is a battle ground. And fashion is my weapon.

Then, as we were tired looking for weapons to be used in this dangerous world, we thought of killing the remaining tic tacs in Broad street, by the canal.
