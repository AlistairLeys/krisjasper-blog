I watched Teen Pinoy Big Brother last night. Big Brother should be a show about survival, about living in a simple/basic household, and studying how people interact with one another while experiencing various moods and behaviours while stressed and pressured. But I noticed that for the last few seasons, it's no longer the housemate's personalities that have brought them inside the house but they have turned PBB into a talent and discovery show.


There was no ugly housemate last night. Most of them, if not all, look like models. So what are they going to do inside? I watch Big Brother series (both UK and RP) because of the arguments, the crying, the threats and the depression factors that they show the televiewers, but now what will I expect from these teen housemates? Modeling? Sweet nothings? So no more dramas? No more knife being pinpointed to another housemate while threatening them? No more bitching? No more quotable quotes? I hope this series wont be boring. Im sure they wont care if they win or not, they just want to be moviestars. I usually support those from Bacolod (even though somebody from Birmingham, UK entered the 2nd season last year). But I was surprised last night when they entered two housemates from two rival schools. La Salle and Ateneo. There's no Ateneo in Bacolod so I really didn't experience the the competition when I was in the UNI. I only feel it if I watch UAAP. But even so, I'm a La Sallian nurse, so I'll support that housemate from La Salle.


Animo La Salle!
