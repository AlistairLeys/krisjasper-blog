Good that I can now watch online the Regional shows of ABSCBN. I watched TV Patrol Bacolod and the Morning Show via ABSCBN Online after publishing my post last night (or early this morning). When I was still in RP, I thought it's so anal to watch these shows, but now, I do watch them. It's a good way of knowing what's new in Bacolod, & Negros in general.

It's just like being in Bacolod.


Now I know how much bangus is in Burgos, Libertad and Central Markets.
And the plans of Ceneco and Baciwa this year. What's new with Lopue's, SM, Gaisano and Robinson's Malls, and other Department stores. What are the new cafe's and restos.
And whoever was gin- lugus (raped) and ginlut-hang (killed)sa Brgy ____, ciudad sang _____.


SEE? I am updated.

Because I zonked at around 3am, I wasn't able to go to the gym at 8am (the usual time I go). I usually go to the gym around that time or @ 730pm onwards. I always think that people who go to the gym from 10am til 3pm are people who do not have a job, and I do not want to be branded that way (even though I think it's only me who thinks it that way anyway). I went there half -heartedly @ 12 noon. It was not really packed, but the smell was just horrible. I don't know from where, but it was awful! And what I dont like is when a stranger starts talking to me. I'm a bit autistic, remember? LOL. I only talk to people I know. (not unless we are in Blogworld) And today, I can't be bothered to talk to anyone anyway. So I just pretended, I was actually listening to my music while he was mumbling about something. I think I've burned like 600 calories today... But when I passed by the OVEN's bake shop I bought 4 cream and custard filled cakes and 2 lemon cupcakes (we'll have them as desserts for tonight)... uhm, I could not resist it... Maybe I'll just chew them later, and then spit them all out. That way I won't really gain a lot of calories.

While typing this, I'm actually watching the Disney Channel, I'm waiting for my roasts- parsnips and potatoes (with chilli and garlic Powder) together with the chicken marinated in oyster sauce. Then later, I'll fry some sprouts with minced garlic.
See? Who said I can't multi task? I'm doing the dinner as both of us are on longday tomorrow, so it's pre Valentine. I dont really celebrate it, but at least there's something I can share if somebody asks me what I did for V-day. But come on, who care really?

I'll be busy for the next days coming. Aside from work, our good friend Jen has given birth only this morning. So we'll visit her on Saturday.

That means I might update this on Monday already.

Happy Valentine's Day EveryOne!

Goodbye!
