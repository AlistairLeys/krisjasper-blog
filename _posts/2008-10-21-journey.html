This is Kris Jasper actually talking:
Not been here for a while, so Im just doing some catching up.
I failed in "something" this week. A is blaming my blog/site due to the time I "wasted" in it. Apparently, I have spent a lot of time maintaining this site and the posts I published for this site. Thinking of posts to publish is actually like choosing clothes to wear. If it is boring and home to chunks of bunkum, no one will bother.
It is actually hard to maintain KrisJasper.com. This is my online diary and I dont want my posts to be repetitive as all I do is go to my workplace (to eat sweets), moan, go to the gym (to commit suicide), moan, spend time in the mall (what else?) and preferably in Broad street (restos and movies) if A and the weather allow, moan, help the needy, meet dignitaries, swallow tablets and moan.


A is right. I wont debate the fact that I do spend time in here. But it is actually not a lot! I spend more time sleeping than doing my blogsite. And I sleep an average of 5hrs/night! I spend more time eating than fiddling things in this site! And I rarely eat. I spend more time praying than being in this site!
What I will dispute is when A said that the time I spent/spend for this site is a "WASTE of time"! I dont think there was a need to justify why I maintain this site, but for it to be blamed for the failure I had, I think it is undue. Why? To share and be proud of what I do, what I decide and what I choose in this everyday chaos we call life, hoping that someone out there, be it a little percentage of this world's population, will learn and be inspired. I do have value in myself, and this site is myself, so how can it be classified as waste/crap? I dont think I am the most successful person in here, but I am also not saying that I live and breathe in wasteland.

Nevertheless, I have failed and I have no other option but to try again.
I have been judged... Unfortunately, my elucidation goes to deaf ears. But in case, just in case you "accidentally" read this coz you are "not one" of my daily visitors as you have claimed, that my posts dont make you smile, that my site doesnt make you proud...
DONT HATE ME,
but I am NOT shutting it down!
(yet).
