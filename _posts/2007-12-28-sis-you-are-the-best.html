There are years lost behind us,
full of words we never spoke
and now we have our separate lives
and less time to share together.
But in my heart I have always felt
the love we have,
and neither distance nor time
can take away the bond
we forged as children...

Growing up, we played and fought,
talked and laughed.
Beyond the fun and games,
beyond the battles we waged,
we found in each other unending support
and a lifelong friend.
Through thick and thin, and difficult times,
we emerged together as one,
protecting each other from the world.
I know that either of us
ever needs anything,
we can count on the other to give their all
and help in every way possible.
Perhaps words are not always
necessary in our relationship,
because we both know what's there.
But today I want to take the time
to remind you how much I really care
and thank you for all
that you have been to me.
I could never ask for, want,
or need a better baby sister,
because I have the best in you.
I love you!

- Erin N. Himelrick

You will always be my baby sister, whatever our ages are... in whatever circumstances we will be. And regardless of what happened, I am still very proud of you.

You are already 22 y.o., a licensed nurse and in love. And I can sense that he loves and cares for you too. He is a good man, and appears like he's from a good family. You are both lucky to have each other, I saw that when I was with you two.

If I'll be VERY honest, yes, I was a bit disappointed. I have planned a lot of things for you... but I forgot that you have your own plans, and you have to design your own life.

I am happy though, coz I know it's no longer just Dad and me as your protectors, now you have him and your baby. I have also gained a new brother, and a baby is joining us on Summer. Our family is again slowly expanding.

Enlightening us about your situation one hour before Christmas, will forever be the bestest Christmas memory I'll have in this lifetime.

I love you sis, Congrats and Happy Birthday!

See you in the Carribean then? just in case you decide to move there...
