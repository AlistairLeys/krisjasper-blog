This will be short and sweet.

1. My sister's flying to St. Marten's next week and will have a 23hours stopover in Amsterdam. If only we knew that that would be the case, I could have planned to meet her (and her husband) in Amsterdam. It's very easy to travel to Amsterdam from UK. I have been there twice and it took me only an hour to get there. Unfortunately it'll be next Thursday, and I havent arranged my life yet. Sigh..... BUT I WILL SURELY (sure with a hope. lol) VISIT YOU IN THE CARRIBEANS NEXT YEAR SIS!

2. I went somewhere this week and three PhysioTherapists were discussing a patient's case. They turned to me and asked if I want them to interprete what they were blabbing about so that I could easily understand. I said "NO THANKS". What do they think of me? Imbecile? For everyone's attention I am sleeping with a PhysioTherapist so I know where sphenoid bone is and what subscapularis muscle's for!


3. I am procrastinating at the moment. It all started last Saturday, and I couldnt be bothered to finish anything. Well, let's face it... This week, whatever I do is constantly wrong anyway. Be it mental, emotional, physical tasks... so I opted to just stop, made myself calm, and I'll continue "those things" when I feel ok.

And after four days, Im beginning to perk up.
